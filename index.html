About Us

On-Site Engineering provides hands-on site engineering, quality control, and construction support services across commercial and residential projects.

We focus on ensuring works are built correctly, documented accurately, and delivered efficiently — reducing risk, rework, and delays on site.

Services

Site Supervision & Technical Support

Hands-on site supervision to ensure works are built right the first time, with clear technical guidance and fast resolution of on-site issues.

  • Verification against drawings and specifications
  • Technical support for contractors
  • Early identification of buildability challenges
  • Stakeholder coordination
  • Ongoing quality and safety support

Quality Control & Inspections

Proactive inspections and quality checks to prevent defects, reduce rework, and maintain compliance.

  • Inspection and Test Plans (ITPs)
  • Identification of quality risks
  • Non-conformance management
  • Documentation and certification support

Building Services Co-ordination

Ensuring services are integrated efficiently to support smooth installation and coordinated sequencing across trades.

  • Services layout review
  • Clash detection and resolution
  • Shop drawing support
  • Installation sequencing coordination

Quality Management (QA/QC)

Structured systems and defect management to maintain accountability and project confidence.

  • QA/QC plan development
  • Inspection management
  • Defect tracking
  • Audit and handover support

Administrative Support & Site Management

Integrated administration and document control to keep communication organised and projects on track.

  • Document control and registers
  • Meeting coordination
  • Tracking approvals and responses
  • Handover documentation support
